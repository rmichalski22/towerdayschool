Learning Environments

Tower Day School is located inside the Centre Congregational Church, the large white church facing the Lynnfield Common. We have seven large and bright separate classrooms on two floors. The high ceilings and the large paned windows help to contribute to the overall feeling of spaciousness.

The preschool classes are capped at twelve students with a teacher and a teaching assistant. We feel that a child’s school performance is significantly related to class size and we work very hard to try to maintain a class size between ten and twelve children. The class groupings remain constant throughout the academic year.

Tower Day offers the students many varied activities. We are well stocked with  learning equipment as well art, music and creative play activities.

Outside, we have a schooner which provides opportunities for climbing, jumping, balance, and social play. Direct attention is paid to the children’s motor development, including their ability to run, jump, hop, skip, and kick and throw a ball. Each class has an assigned time on the playground so the children have easy access to the equipment.
