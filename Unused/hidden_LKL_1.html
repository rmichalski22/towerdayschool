Tower Day School provides a warm and inviting atmosphere in which children may begin developing social and learning skills.

Tower Day School is a part of the Centre Congregational Church. As such, its governing board is composed of representatives of various committees of the church, as well as the Pastor and the Tower Day School Director. Tower Day School is licensed by the Department of Early Education and Care.

Tower Day School operates two separate half-day sessions; a morning session from 8:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m., and an afternoon session from 12 noon to 3:00 p.m.  There is also an extended day session offered from 11:30 a.m. until 3:00 p.m.  Our full-day classes operate from 8:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.

Directions:

From I-95 / Route 128, take exit 41 (Main Street Lynnfield).
Turn right at the bottom of the ramp onto Main Street, and follow Main Street for about 1.5 miles.  Tower Day School & the Centre Congregational Church are located on the right, at the corner of Main Street & Summer Street, immediately after the common.   Parking is available off Main Street behind the church.
