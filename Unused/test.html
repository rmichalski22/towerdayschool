The school year is 35 weeks, following an academic year calendar. Tuition is based on a yearly fee. It may be paid in full, quarterly, or in nine equal payments. The tuition for the 2011/2012 academic year is:

  • 2 day class $2,349
  • 3 day class $3,510
  • 3 day full day (8:30 – 2:30) class $6,120
  • 5 day class $5,022
  • Transition class $6,372
  • Kindergarten $8,235


Please read our Application Information Sheet and Tuition Schedule before filling out an Application Form.

We begin accepting application forms for enrollment in January for classes starting the following September. Please call the Tower Day School Office if you would like to make an appointment to visit the school. Visits are conducted by the Director or other staff members and are about 15-20 minutes during October, November and December. In order to respect the confidentiality and safety of the children enrolled at the school, visitors should not expect to tour the school unescorted.
