Please call the Tower Day School Office at 781-334-5576 or email to towerdayschool@gmail.com in the Fall for a tour of the school. Tours are conducted by the Director or other staff members and are about 20 minutes. In order to respect the confidentiality and safety of the children enrolled at the school, visitors should not expect to tour the school unescorted.

Tower Day School follows an Academic Year Calendar with children being enrolled from September to June. Applications for enrollment become available in December for the following September.
