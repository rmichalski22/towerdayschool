SMS Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

Tower Day School ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide when you opt in to receive SMS text messages from Tower Day School.

What Information We Collect

When you opt in to our SMS notification service, we collect your mobile phone number and your name for the purpose of sending you school-related text messages.

How We Use Your Information

We use your mobile phone number solely to send you school notifications, including but not limited to weather closures, early dismissals, schedule changes, and other operational announcements related to Tower Day School.

Sharing of Information

We will not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or any personal information collected through our SMS program with third parties for promotional or marketing purposes.

All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties. We may share your personal data, including your SMS opt-in or consent status, with third parties that help us provide our messaging services, including but not limited to platform providers, phone companies, and any other vendors who assist us in the delivery of text messages.

Message Frequency and Rates

Message frequency varies based on school events and conditions. Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. After opting out, you will receive a final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

Help

For help or questions about our SMS program, reply HELP to any message you receive from us, or contact us at office@towerdayschool.org.
