Tower Day School is located at 5 Summer Street, inside Centre Congregational Church, the large white church facing the Lynnfield Common.

From I-95 / Route 128, take exit 59 (Main Street Lynnfield).
Turn right at the bottom of the ramp onto Main Street, and follow Main Street for about 1.5 miles.  Tower Day School & Centre Congregational Church are located on the right, at the corner of Main Street & Summer Street, immediately after the common.
Parking is available off Main Street behind the church.
